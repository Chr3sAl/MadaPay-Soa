MadaPay Soa Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: 2026-03-13

If you want your Messenger and related transaction data deleted from MadaPay Soa systems, please follow one of the methods below.

Method A: Request by Email

  1. Send an email to christian.t3ky@gmail.com.
  2. Subject line: Data Deletion Request.
  3. Include your Messenger profile name and, if possible, a screenshot of your chat with the page.

Method B: Request in Messenger

  1. Message the page and type: DELETE MY DATA.
  2. Our support team will verify ownership and process your request.

Processing Time

We target completion within 7 business days, unless a longer period is required by law.

What Gets Deleted

  • Messenger session data and conversation metadata associated with your account.
  • Stored identifiers used for support and workflow tracking, where legally permitted.
