MadaPay Soa Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-13

MadaPay Soa (“we”, “our”, “us”) provides Messenger-based financial assistance and exchange workflow support. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how users can request deletion.

1. Data We Collect

  • Messenger identifiers (PSID) and chat messages sent to our page.
  • Language preference and conversation state used to continue bot flows.
  • Transaction-related information you provide (for example amount, transfer ID, and status).
  • Technical logs required for service reliability and security.

2. Why We Use Data

  • To provide customer support and respond to requests through Messenger.
  • To process and verify transfer workflows.
  • To detect abuse, prevent fraud, and improve service quality.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be shared only with service providers and partners required to operate the payment/verification workflow, or when legally required.

4. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed for support, transaction processing, compliance, and legal obligations.

5. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.
